# 🥇 Gold Layer - Analytics

## 📌 Overview
The Gold layer represents the final, business-ready data used for analytics and reporting. This layer aggregates and transforms data from the Silver layer to generate insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Provide clean, aggregated datasets
- Support dashboards and reporting
- Enable business decision-making

---

## 📊 Sample Analytics Use Cases

### 1. Trips per Day
- Total number of trips per day
- Helps identify demand patterns

### 2. Weekend vs Weekday Analysis
- Compare trip volume between weekdays and weekends
- Understand peak usage behavior

### 3. Holiday Impact Analysis
- Analyze trip trends during holidays
- Identify seasonal demand spikes

---

## ⚙️ Transformations
- Aggregations (SUM, COUNT)
- Grouping by date, city, or category
- Joining with calendar dimension

---

## 🔄 Data Source
- Input: Silver Layer
- Output: Aggregated tables for dashboards

---

## 📈 Output
- Used in dashboards (Power BI / Tableau)
- Supports business insights and reporting
